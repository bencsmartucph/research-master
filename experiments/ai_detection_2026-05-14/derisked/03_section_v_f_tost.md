The models of support for sharing wealth bear out the lopsided guess from §III. They do so where we most needed the even-handed tale to fail. RTI foretells somewhat more backing for sharing across all regimes (β=0.041, p<0.001). We find no such swing across regimes on the side of fellowship. The cross-regime shift that drives the shutting-out finding does not show up here; the RTI × Liberal term is small and not meaningful (β=0.013, SE=0.019, p=0.488). The setting of welfare bodies cleanly weakens how weakness turns into shutting out. It does not, so far as we can tell, weaken how the same weakness turns into fellowship in sharing. That is the lopsidedness. It is more than a missing finding; it is what we foretold in §III.F (P2). The even-handed shielding tale would have to deny it.

The ISSP 2006 Role of Government unit (N=10,216, 12 lands) gives us a second test. A different sample; a different outcome; a different stretch of years. The upshot: no link between openness to machines and welfare breadth on backing for jobless spending (β=+0.010, SE=0.016, p=0.55). A second null. The 2006 data come years before Frey and Osborne (2017) spoke of machines taking work. We read the shielding pathway as needing openness to machines to stand out as a threat to one's kind in shared talk. Only then can fellowship be rallied around it. The harm pathway holds no such need. The lopsidedness shows up across stretches of time; the underlying bodily weaknesses themselves shift.

A reading of the sharing-null as a measuring hitch is open to us. The data lets us speak with more sharpness than the bare null itself. A TOST test of sameness against a smallest meaningful size matched to the shutting-out side (|β|=0.06; the worth the even-handed tale would have foretold) yields p_TOST = 0.008 for the ESS sharing link and p_TOST = 0.001 for the ISSP backup test (both at α=0.05). The 90% CIs lie wholly within ±0.05. The sharing-null does not merely fail to find a link; it rules out a steering of the size the even-handed tale would foretell. A small shielding link (|β|<0.03) stays within our reach to see; yet we can set aside the even-handed guess.
